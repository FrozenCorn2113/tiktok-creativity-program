# The TikTok Viral Formula: What Actually Works in 2026

Virality isn’t random. It’s a predictable mix of hook, retention, and shareability. This guide explains the formula that consistently drives views.

## The viral formula

1. **Hook:** A specific, relevant promise in the first 2 seconds.  
2. **Retention:** Fast pacing, quick cuts, and one clear story.  
3. **Replay:** A twist or payoff that makes people rewatch.  
4. **Shareability:** A takeaway that people want to share.
