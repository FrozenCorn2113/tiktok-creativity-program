Repurposing extends the life of every video. This guide shows how to reuse content without hurting performance.

## Repurposing workflow

1. Post TikTok first
2. Export, remove watermark
3. Adapt captions for Reels/Shorts
4. Track performance differences

## AI clipping tools accelerate the whole process

The manual version of this workflow (watch the source video, find the moments, cut each clip by hand) takes hours per video. AI clipping tools collapse that to 20 to 30 minutes.

Both <AffiliateLink slug="opusclip">OpusClip</AffiliateLink> and <AffiliateLink slug="vizard">Vizard.ai</AffiliateLink> accept YouTube URLs directly. Paste the URL, and within minutes you have 10 to 25 clips pre-formatted for TikTok's 9:16 format with captions applied. You review, keep the ones worth posting, and move on.

For creators repurposing long-form content (YouTube episodes, podcast recordings, webinars), this is where the ROI calculation flips. A 60-minute source video that would have taken 3 hours to clip manually now takes 30 minutes. That's the time leverage that makes consistent cross-platform posting realistic.
