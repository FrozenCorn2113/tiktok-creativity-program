# TikTok Creator Rewards Disabled: How to Get Reinstated

If your Creator Rewards access is disabled, don’t panic. Most cases are fixable when you correct violations and reapply.

<CalloutBox type="info" title="Quick facts">
  <ul>
    <li>Disabling is often tied to community guideline issues or ineligible content.</li>
    <li>Reinstatement requires proof of compliance.</li>
    <li>Consistency after reinstatement is crucial.</li>
  </ul>
</CalloutBox>

## Deliverable summary

**Purpose:** Help creators regain Creator Rewards access.  
**Target Keywords:** TikTok Creator Rewards disabled, Creator Rewards reinstated.  
**User Intent:** Understand disablement and reapply successfully.  
**Success Metrics:** Lower disablement recurrence, time on page, email signups.  
**Next Steps:** Fix violations and submit reinstatement request.

## Common reasons for disablement

1. Repeated community guideline violations  
2. Ineligible content formats  
3. Reused or unoriginal content  
4. Region or account type issues  

## Reinstatement steps

1. Review violations in your account status.  
2. Remove or archive flagged content.  
3. Post new, original 1+ minute content.  
4. Submit a reinstatement request.
