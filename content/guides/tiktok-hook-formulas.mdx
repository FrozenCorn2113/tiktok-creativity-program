Hooks decide whether a viewer keeps watching. Use these formulas to improve retention and qualified views.

## 20 hook formulas

1. “If you’re [target], this will save you.”

6. “Most creators miss this.”  
7. “Here’s the fastest way to [result].”  
8. “I tested [strategy] so you don’t have to.”  
9. “The mistake that cost me [result].”  
10. “This changed my RPM overnight.”

12. “3 reasons you’re not getting qualified views.”  
13. “The tool I use for [task].”  
14. “My exact process for [result].”  
15. “You’re doing this wrong.”  
16. “What I’d do if I started over today.”  
17. “Save this for later.”  
18. “The one thing TikTok rewards most.”  
19. “I tried this for 7 days.”  
20. “Here’s the proof.”
