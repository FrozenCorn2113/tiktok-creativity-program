# Best TikTok Posting Schedule for Maximum Views

Consistency wins on TikTok, but posting too much can burn you out. This guide provides a sustainable schedule that keeps performance up.

<CalloutBox type="info" title="Quick facts">
  <ul>
    <li>3–5 posts per week is a strong baseline.</li>
    <li>Follow-up videos increase retention and saves.</li>
    <li>Consistency matters more than volume.</li>
  </ul>
</CalloutBox>

## Deliverable summary

**Purpose:** Help creators build a sustainable posting cadence.  
**Target Keywords:** best TikTok posting schedule, TikTok posting cadence.  
**User Intent:** Find a schedule that grows views without burnout.  
**Success Metrics:** Retention, average views per post, email signups.  
**Next Steps:** Commit to a 4-week schedule and track results.

## Recommended schedule

- 3–4 posts per week  
- 1 follow-up video  
- 1 experiment  
- 1 core series post  

## Best times to post

Start with:
- 6–9pm local time  
- Weekdays for educational content  
- Weekends for entertainment
