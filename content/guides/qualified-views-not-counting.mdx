# Qualified Views Not Counting? Here’s Why + Fixes

Qualified views are the foundation of Creator Rewards earnings. If yours are low, the fix is usually straightforward. Use this checklist to find the problem and correct it fast.

<CalloutBox type="info" title="Quick facts">
  <ul>
    <li>Only For You Page views count, and only on 1+ minute videos.</li>
    <li>Duets, Stitches, Photo Mode, and reuploads don’t qualify.</li>
    <li>Views only count after you join the program.</li>
  </ul>
</CalloutBox>

## Deliverable summary

**Purpose:** Help creators fix missing qualified views and restore earnings.  
**Target Keywords:** TikTok qualified views not counting, qualified views low.  
**User Intent:** Identify why views are missing and how to fix it.  
**Success Metrics:** Lower drop-offs, fewer support questions, better RPM stability.  
**Next Steps:** Run the checklist and post three compliant videos.

## 1. Your video is under 60 seconds

Only 1+ minute videos qualify. This is the most common mistake.

## 2. The format is ineligible

Duets, Stitches, and Photo Mode are not eligible for qualified views.

## 3. The video was posted before approval

Only videos posted **after** you join the program count.

## 4. View source is not For You

Views from profile, search, or shared links don’t count the same way.

## 5. Region mismatch

If your audience is primarily outside eligible countries, qualified views will be lower.

## Diagnostic checklist

1. Confirm video length (60+ seconds).  
2. Confirm post date (after approval).  
3. Confirm format (no Duet/Stitch/Photo Mode).  
4. Check audience region breakdown.  
5. Review completion rate and engagement.
